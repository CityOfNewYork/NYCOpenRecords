For requests for agency records pursuant to the Freedom of Information Law (FOIL), first try searching our contract documents database. All other requests must be submitted in writing to our FOIL Records Access Officer. Requests can be mailed or emailed. When available, the following information should be included in the FOIL request:

  • Location details
  • Date of accident (if applicable)
  • Type(s) of record(s) requested
  • DDC project number

Please remember, the more specific the FOIL request, the easier it will be for us to perform an expedient search and to locate responsive and relevant documentation. Please note that FOIL requests are intended for obtaining records and are not suitable for comments or questions requiring a response.

The statutory fee for the reproduction of agency records is twenty-five cents ($0.25) per standard sized copy and actual reproduction cost for all other copies. Please include the agency's FOIL Number (FOIL No.) as indicated above on any and all future correspondence on this matter. If you have any questions regarding your request, please contact the agency's FOIL office at (718) 391-1701.
